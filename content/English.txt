What Makes Our Courses At EACC Unique?
We believe in learning by doing. Our classes are dynamic, interactive, and tailored to your personal learning goals. ​​

With EACC You’ll build your speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills through real-life practice, lively discussions, and engaging topics that matter to you.

Each level is carefully designed to match the internationally recognized CEFR framework, from complete beginner to advanced.


Advanced Conversation

A course designed by EACC for confident, advanced English learners. If you're aiming to boost your fluency, this is the perfect course for you. It’s structured to take you from an intermediate level all the way to advanced.

​

By the end of the course, you’ll be able to understand nearly everything you hear or read with ease, summarize information from various sources clearly and effectively, and express yourself with accuracy even in complex situations.

The course is divided into 8 levels.

Each sub-level includes:

24 hours of practice

Access to a Learning Management System (LMS) to track your progress, complete exercises, and monitor your improvement step by step​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

​​Interactive exercises like Mini Shadowing, Role Plays, Discussions, and Real-life Situations — so you can practice real conversations and speak fluently without fear

Kids for Peace
Creative Course Content

We introduce English through eight engaging themes using a “Big Book” approach that keeps learning fun and varied. Children explore language through stories, songs, games, and activities that support both cognitive and social development.

Kids with Capes
 Building Digital Literacy and Fine Motor Skills

Our course promotes early digital literacy while enhancing fine motor skills. With interactive, child-friendly online resources, learners can enjoy educational games and practice on tablets, laptops, or mobile devices.

Kids Playing Tug of War

Through the Content and Language Integrated Learning (CLIL) approach, we introduce real-world topics such as nature and the environment, helping children connect English to the world around them in a meaningful way.
