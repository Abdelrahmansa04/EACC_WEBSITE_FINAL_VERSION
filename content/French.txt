At the Egyptian American Center Alexandria, we are proud to offer a dynamic and structured French language curriculum tailored to learners of all ages and levels. For Beginners
(A1 - A2 Stages)
A1 Level: Learners will be able to introduce themselves, form basic sentences, read and understand simple texts, and engage in short conversations. The focus is on listening comprehension, vocabulary building, and the foundations of communication.

A2 Level: Students expand their ability to express themselves in more structured ways, covering daily interactions and basic topics across oral, written, listening, and grammar components.

Arch of Triumph
Intermediate Level
(B1 Stage)
Students at this stage will:

Discuss a wide range of topics using an expanded vocabulary.

Understand various types of listening materials and videos.

Participate in discussions based on these resources to strengthen comprehension and speaking fluency.

Eiffel Tower Paris
Advanced Level
(C Stage)
Learners will be equipped to handle advanced conversations, using diverse vocabulary with flexibility and depth.

Emphasis is placed on mastering complex listening and speaking skills across varied themes and real-world contexts.
