A New Dimension in Educational Placement

At EACC, we believe that effective learning begins with self-understanding.

That is why psychological assessment is integrated into our placement process alongside language testing. In addition to measuring language proficiency, we explore how each learner thinks, learns, and responds to challenges so we can provide more personalized recommendations and better support.

When people understand themselves, they are better equipped to learn effectively, make confident decisions, and reach their full potential.
