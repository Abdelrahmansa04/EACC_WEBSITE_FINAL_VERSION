Mindspace Center

About the Center:
A center specialized in supporting children and developing their educational and psychological abilities. It works with children who face learning difficulties, academic delay, or behavioral and educational challenges.
We believe that every child has real potential, but sometimes needs a different learning method that suits their abilities and takes their psychological state into account. That is why we provide a safe and supportive environment that helps the child learn with confidence and calm, away from pressure or comparison.

Because every child can learn... when we choose the right path for them.

Our Specialized Services

Learning difficulties and academic delay:
- Reading, writing, and math difficulties.
- Weak attention and memory.
- Individual educational programs based on assessment results.
- Improving academic performance and increasing motivation to learn.
- Periodic follow-up reports to monitor the case and measure progress.

Skills development:
- Developing attention and concentration.
- Strengthening memory.
- Developing language skills.
- Developing basic academic skills.
- Improving visual-motor coordination.
- Reducing hyperactivity and attention deficit (ADHD).

Speech and language sessions:
- Improving pronunciation of sounds and letters.
- Developing language comprehension and expression.
- Increasing vocabulary.
- Improving verbal and non-verbal communication.
- Periodic follow-up reports to monitor the case and measure progress.

Behavior modification:
- Analyzing behavior and understanding its real causes.
- Teaching positive alternative behavior instead of prevention and punishment.
- Reinforcing desired behavior with encouragement methods suitable for the child's age.
- Helping the child regulate emotions and express them in a healthy way.
- Helping the child manage hyperactivity and attention deficit.
- Effective cooperation with the family to ensure continuity.

Family guidance helps the family with:
- Learning difficulties (reading - writing - math).
- Weak attention and memory.
- Developmental and academic skills development.
- Speech and language sessions.
- Behavior modification.
- Psychological support and building self-confidence.

Our work approach:
- Careful assessment before starting any program.
- Identifying the child's strengths and real needs.
- Preparing an individualized plan for each person.
- Gradual training without pressure or comparison.
- Periodic follow-up reports to monitor the case and measure progress.

Comprehensive Assessment:
We begin with a comprehensive assessment using approved scientific tools to measure:
- Intelligence test (Stanford).
- Cognitive abilities.
- Academic skills.
- Attention and memory.
- Language skills.
- Behavioral and emotional aspects.
Based on the assessment results, an individualized intervention plan is prepared to suit the child's needs and abilities.

Our Partnership with the Family:
We consider the family an essential partner in the child's development journey, and we work on:
- Guiding the family in correct handling methods.
- Following up on the plan implementation at home.
- Creating integration between the center, home, and school.

Our Vision:
To enable every child to learn and grow with confidence by choosing the educational path best suited to their abilities and needs.

Mindspace Center

عن المركز:
مركز متخصص في دعم الأطفال وتنمية قدراتهم التعليمية والنفسية، ويعمل مع الأطفال الذين يواجهون صعوبات في التعلم أو التأخر الدراسي أو التحديات السلوكية والتربوية.
نؤمن أن كل طفل لديه إمكانات حقيقية، لكنه أحياناً يحتاج لطريقة تعليم مختلفة تناسب قدراته وتراعي حالته النفسية. لذلك نوفر بيئة آمنة وداعمة تساعد الطفل على التعلم بثقة وهدوء، بعيداً عن الضغط أو المقارنة.

لأن كل طفل يقدر يتعلم... لما نختار له الطريق الصح.

التقييم الشامل:
نبدأ بتقييم شامل باستخدام أدوات علمية معتمدة لقياس:
- اختبار الذكاء (ستانفورد).
- القدرات المعرفية.
- المهارات الأكاديمية.
- الانتباه والذاكرة.
- المهارات اللغوية.
- الجوانب السلوكية والانفعالية.
وبناء على نتائج التقييم، يتم وضع خطة تدخل فردية تتناسب مع احتياجات الطفل وقدراته.

شراكتنا مع الأسرة:
نعتبر الأسرة شريكاً أساسياً في رحلة التطور، ونعمل على:
- توجيه الأسرة لأساليب التعامل الصحيحة.
- متابعة تطبيق الخطة في المنزل.
- تحقيق تكامل بين المركز والبيت والمدرسة.

رؤيتنا:
تمكين كل طفل من التعلم والنمو بثقة، من خلال اختيار الطريق التعليمي الأنسب لقدراته واحتياجاته.
