Privacy Policy

Egyptian American Cultural Center is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This statement explains how we collect, use and protect information submitted through our website and services.

You may visit our website without disclosing personally identifiable information. If you submit information by using our services, requesting information, enrolling in a course or applying for a job, we use it only for the purposes described in this policy.

Personal Information Collection

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Information that you provide when enrolling on a course, requesting a service or applying for a job.
  • Identifying information when you contact us for assistance.
  • Records of email correspondence or messages you send to us.
  • Optional feedback forms used for quality assurance or research purposes.
  • Details of transactions carried out through our site and fulfilment of your orders.
  • Website visit details such as number of visits, time spent on the site, traffic data and related communication data.
  • Personal information such as name, address, email, phone number and student ID number where applicable.

When Do We Collect Information?

We endeavour to collect and use your personal information only with your knowledge and consent, typically when you:

  • Order and subsequently use services.
  • Make customer enquiries.
  • Register for information or other services.
  • Request product or program information.
  • Submit a job application or send a CV.
  • Graduate from our Center or work with us.
  • Respond to communications from us, such as questionnaires or surveys.

Why Do We Use Your Information?

We collect information to process your request or order and to provide the best possible service. We do not keep a record of your credit or debit card information.

If you choose to provide personal information, it will be used to support the intended purposes stated at the time of collection, such as enrolment, communication, service delivery, qualification administration or recruitment.

Information Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse or disclosure. Access is limited to team members who need the information to perform their duties.

For questions about Egyptian American Cultural Center data protection and privacy practices, please email info_eacc@eacc-egy.com.
